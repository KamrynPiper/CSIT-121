Bee and Puppycat

Bee and Puppycat is an animated show featured on Netflix. It stars a mysterious girl and a confusing buddy she takes care of.
The two adventure across planets working temp jobs for some quick cash. I personally love the show and mark it as one of my THREE THINGS
because it is very relaxing, funny, colorful, and creative. Although it ends on an unending note, It's one of the few shows I do not mind rewatching!
