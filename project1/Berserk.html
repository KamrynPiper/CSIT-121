Berserk

Berserk is a manga that I recently started reading. Suprisingly, it is my very first manga that I've started. It depicts the story of one man who has overcome the most horrifying obstacles with
his rage and undeniable vengence towards those who have tormented him and the people he cared about. It is most definitely a book rated for adults, as its content gets very explicit in every volume you read.
I have already spent quite a lot of money on this series and plan to spend more!
