Tres Leches Cake Recipe<3


Recipe Published by Lauren Allen on Tastes Better from Scratch

~Ingredients~

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sugar
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Whole Milk
  • Heavy Whipping Cream

~Prep Steps~

PLEASE NOTE ALL PREP STEP INFORMATION IS FROM: Tastes Better from Scratch

  1. Combine the flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl.
  2. Separate whisked eggs into two separte bowls.
  3. Add 3/4 cup of sugar to the egg yolk bowl and mix until pale yellow. Then add 1/3 cup of milk and vanilla.
  4. Stir to combine, then pour egg yolk mixture into flower.
  5. Gently stir to combine but do not mix.
  6. Use electric beaters to beat the egg whites on high speed. As they begin to whip into stiff peaks, gradually mix in the remaining 1/4 cup of sugar. Fold the stiffly beaten egg whites into the batter gently, scraping the bottom and sides of the bowl, until combined.
  7. Pour batter into pan and smooth it into an even layer. Bake for 25 to 35 minutes.Remove from the oven and allow cake to cool completely.
  8. Combine the evaporated milk, sweetened condensed milk, and whole milk in a small bowl. Once the cake has cooled use a fork to poke holes all over the top of the cake.
  9. Slowly pour the milk mixture over the the top of the cake, making sure to pour near the edges and all around. Refrigerate the cake for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  10. Whip enough heavy cream to cover atop the cake. Once heavy whipping cream is spreaded, add a sprinkle of cinnamon and freshly slices strawberries on top.
